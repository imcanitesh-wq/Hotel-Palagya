Our Story

Where obedience
becomes devotion

Pala+Agya=Palagya

At Hotel Palagya, the name itself is a vow. Pala and Agya together mean obedient — not servile, but devoted. A deep, attentive care that anticipates before you ask, that settles before you arrive.

Positioned at the gateway of the sacred — beside Pashupatinath, the holiest Shiva temple in the world — Palagya is a contemporary sanctuary where pilgrims, families, and travellers find equal welcome.

One kilometre from Tribhuvan International Airport. Five minutes' walk to Pashupatinath. Right in the flow of Kathmandu life.
