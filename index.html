Questions & Answers Everything you need to know before your stay.

Simple answers
to your questions

Hotel Palagya is located in Tilganga, Kathmandu — just five minutes walking distance from the sacred Pashupatinath Temple and Tribhuvan International Airport. It's one of the most convenient locations for both pilgrims and travelers.
We offer six room categories: Standard Family ($19.90), Standard ($23), Special Priest Room ($35), Family Room ($40), Deluxe ($45), and Deluxe Family ($50). All rooms include attached bathroom with 24-hour hot water and free Wi-Fi.
Yes! Hotel Palagya has a 500-seat banquet hall, a rooftop garden restaurant venue, and full conference/seminar setup with AV equipment. We cater for weddings, corporate events, parties and all celebrations with vegetarian and non-vegetarian options.
Our Rooftop Restaurant offers exceptional Nepali and international cuisine, cocktails, wines, and a hookah lounge. Breakfast, cultural lunches and diverse international dinner buffets are prepared by our experienced chefs.
Yes — Hotel Palagya is bookable through Agoda, Expedia, Hotels.com, and TripAdvisor in addition to direct booking by calling us or sending a WhatsApp message. Direct booking often gets you the best rates.
Standard check-in is at 12:00 PM and check-out at 11:00 AM. Our front desk is open 24 hours, 7 days a week for early arrivals or late departures — please contact us in advance so we can best accommodate you.

Still have questions?

Every guest's needs are different. Our team is available 24/7 to help with reservations, special requests, event planning or any question you might have.
