Our Story

A devotion, handed down

For decades, Hotel Palagya has welcomed travellers, pilgrims, and dreamers to the threshold of the sacred.

The Meaning
Pala+Agya=Palagya

The name carries a single, quiet promise: obedience — not servitude, but a willing devotion to the comfort of others. It is the oldest form of Nepali hospitality, where a guest is treated as a manifestation of the divine. Atithi Devo Bhava — the guest is god.

Welcome

Warmth, at its gracious best

Hotel Palagya invites you to experience the warmth of Nepalese hospitality at its most gracious. Bask in the courtesy, comfort, and care that come from a staff with decades of operational experience in some of the finest hotels and restaurants in the country.

Well-appointed suites and rooms, facilities for seminars, conferences, and banquets — all just five minutes' walk from the world-famous Pashupatinath temple and the international airport. Whether you arrive for business, celebration, or simply pleasure, Palagya is the perfect choice.
